Frequently Asked Questions

How do "proofs" count work?

A proof is generated when a customer completes the flow (address + draw + item selection + result). Partial sessions don't count.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts. Cancel before your next billing date. Widget stays live until end of current period.

What if I exceed 100 proofs on Starter?

Widget still works. We'll send an upgrade prompt. If you don't upgrade by month-end, widget shows "Upgrade to continue" banner.

Do you offer discounts for annual billing?

Yes! Pay annually and save 20%. Pro: $950/year (~$79/mo), Multi-Location: $2,870/year (~$239/mo).

Can I use it on multiple websites?

Starter/Pro: 1 domain. Multi-Location: up to 10 domains. Need more? Contact us for enterprise pricing.

Is my customer data private?

Yes. You own all proof artifacts (measurements, addresses, results). We never contact your customers.
